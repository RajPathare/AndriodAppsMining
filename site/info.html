Correlation


Old dataset

there is a -ve correlation between no.of downloads and price of app. So for the success of app, the price should be kept very low or free.

the high positive correlation between downloads and no.of reviews validates that if the downloads increase then the no. of people giving reviews will also increase.

the negative correlation between price and rating indicates that price may affect the rating of the app. free apps tend to get more high ratings.

New dataset

the positive correlation editors-choice,reviews and editors-choice,installs specify that if a app is selected as editors-choice it will lead to more reviews and installs.

screenshots play an important role. positive correlation between screenshots and reviews indicated, people will review apps that have more screenshots.

screenshots-iap indicate that in-app purchases will increase if we increase screenshots as the purchase options are displayed through images only.

correlation between free and installs indicate, free apps are more popular than paid apps.

it can be seen that apps having in-app purchases usually give more reviews compared to normal apps.
